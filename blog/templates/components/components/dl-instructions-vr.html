VR - Oculus Go / Quest Instructions VR

  1. Open the Oculus app on your mobile phone (make sure to have wifi, bluetooth and location enabled).
  2. Tap "Settings" in the bottom menu.
  3. Under Payment, tap "Redeem Code".
  4. Enter the redemption code provided by Sisu VR with your purchase and tap submit.
  5. In your headset, open the Apps menu.
  6. In the tab in the upper right corner of the menu, select "Not Installed."
  7. The new app should now appear. Click the ENP app and select download.

VR - Steam Instructions VR

  1. Launch the Steam app on your VR ready Desktop or Laptop.
  2. In the top navigation menu, under "Games" select Activate a product on Steam.
  3. Click "Next" to be prompted to enter the product code.
  4. Enter the redemption code provided by Sisu VR with your purchase and click "Next."
  5. You will be prompted to download and install the app.
