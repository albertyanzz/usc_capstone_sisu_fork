Frequently Asked Questions
Why Sisu VR?

Sisu VR's training program, Empower Now, is an immersive workplace conduct training experience, employing cutting-edge ethical and psychological research and virtual reality (VR) technology. Through Empower Now, employees will learn how to address misconduct through simulated incidents of harassment, bullying, or discrimination. Post-training, employers will obtain a comprehensive report on methods to improve their organization, as necessary. Would you like to create a kinder, more respectful, and courageous workplace?

We're excited to work with you, so contact us today!

Why virtual reality (VR)?

Currently, most training solutions are delivered via videos, live presentations, or manuals. However, these methods have been proven to be ineffective. On the other hand, studies show VR soft skills training promotes better and faster learning, higher retention of information, and increased engagement. It has also been shown to bolster empathy. Sisu VR delivers high-quality experiences in multiple first-person perspectives using VR. By interacting with realistic scenarios, employees are empowered to effectively address incidents of misconduct.
