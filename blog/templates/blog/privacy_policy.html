Website Visitors

Sisu's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Sisu's visitors use its website. From time to time, Sisu may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Sisu also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on www.sisuvr.com blog posts. Sisu only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below.

The security of your Personal Information is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Cookies

To enrich and perfect your online experience, Sisu uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Sisu uses cookies to help Sisu identify and track visitors, their usage of www.sisuvr.com, and their website access preferences. Sisu visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Sisu's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Sisu's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to Sisu's use of cookies.
